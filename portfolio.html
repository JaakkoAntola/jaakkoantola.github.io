Game Music

Behemoth - This exhilarating demo track was composed for the game Behemoth, a 2D space shooter created during a university C# programming course. With energetic rhythms and high-tech space vibes, the music features floating elements and disorienting effects that perfectly complement the game's fast-paced action. Listen and experience the dynamic range of emotions as you navigate through the cosmos.

Build Deities - Inspired by the classic strategy games Age of Empires and Civilization, this demo track showcases the intense, warlike atmosphere of Build Deities, a 2D PvP strategy game designed during a university game design course. Combining the sounds of war drums, imminent danger, and a fusion of diverse cultural elements, the music transports players into a world of epic battles and strategic conquests. Immerse yourself in the rich soundscape of this captivating track.

Other Audio

J. Antola - Fantasy op. 3 no. 1: This captivating piano composition is one of my earliest works, written when I was just 12 years old. The recording features a youthful me performing the piece in the same year it was composed. This enchanting piece is a testament to my lifelong passion for music and my dedication to honing my skills as a pianist.

LoL: A unique and experimental piece, LoL was one of my initial forays into using Ableton Live for music production. Drawing inspiration from the rhythmic patterns of Morse code, the piece incorporates the code for "LOL" as its central motif. Though created primarily as a practice exercise and not fully polished, LoL showcases my willingness to explore new musical ideas and push the boundaries of my creativity.
